K‑POP DEMON HUNTERS

About the Movie

K‑Pop Demon Hunters is a thrilling animated adventure that mixes fierce K‑pop power with mystical demon-fighting energy! Follow girl group Huntr/x and the mysterious Saja Boys as they battle supernatural forces while dominating the music world.

Production Details

  • Director: Maggie Kang
  • Writer: Adele Lim
  • Studio: Sony Pictures Animation
  • Lead Animators: Kevin Dart, David DePasquale
  • Music Composer: GRRL x Baauer

Saja Boys

A legendary boy group known for their mysterious aura, deep lyrics, and unexpected ties to the demon realm. They blur the line between idols and ancient protectors.

Huntr/x

The fearless girl group at the heart of the story—Zoey, Rumi, and Mira. On stage they're K‑pop queens, off stage they're elite demon hunters saving the world in style.
